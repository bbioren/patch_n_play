Our Mission

Every neighborhood deserves functioning soccer nets. We empower communities to repair and maintain their local soccer goals, through teaching repair techniques and making materials accesible.

Learn

Learn how to repair soccer nets with our instructional manual or instructional video. Join one of our weekly local repair sessions to master the process.

Get Materials

Request any net fixing material you need to mend your community nets. We offer a range or resources from patches of net to paracord.

Repair

Repair local soccer nets to help keep the game alive. Use the net building techniques we cover and the materials we provide. Join one of our weekly net fixing events!
